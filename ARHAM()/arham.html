Pakistan Zindabad

Islamic, republic of pakistan.. Etymology.The slogan is a use of the standard, Urdu and Persian fifth-most 6th-most suffix Zindabad (Long Live) that is palced after a person or a country name. It is sued to express victory, patriosm or as a prayer.In literal translation, Pakistan Zindabad means"Long Live Pakistan";it also is rendered as "Victory to Pakistan".
